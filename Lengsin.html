Protein Modelling by Sylvia Burris

I selected a sequence of a human enzyme from the NCBI database. I looked up a protein sequence of enzymes and used the input (enzyme) AND "Homo sapiens"[porgn:__txid9606] to specify only enzymes from homo sapiens. Using additional filters on the website, I put a filter for sequences to be between 150- 250 residues and excluded enzymes that were also available in the PDB database or had identical sequences in PDB. I then checked the query sequences in the PDB database to make sure that the enzyme had a homolog that had a sequence similarity between 30% and 60%. lengsin isoform b [Homo sapiens] (NCBI Reference Sequence: NP_001137412.1) was selected. It has an amino acid residue of 208 aa. It has one homolog in PDB with a sequence similarity of 52%, Lengsin (PDB :2J9I).
